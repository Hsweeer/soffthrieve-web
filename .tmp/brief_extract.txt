1. Background and Context
Softhrive is launching Meta ad campaigns targeting Saudi Arabia and Qatar for Shopify store 
development and mobile app development services. The ads use Meta Instant Lead Forms so visitors do 
not land on the website directly from ads. However Saudi clients will Google Softhrive after seeing the ad 
to verify credibility before filling the form or after filling it. The website must instantly confirm that Softhrive 
is the right agency for their needs.
Primary Website Purpose for This Campaign
Saudi client sees Arabic Shopify ad → fills Meta lead form → Googles Softhrive to verify credibility 
→ visits website → must immediately see: professional agency, Saudi market experience, real 
portfolio, clear pricing, easy WhatsApp contact.

3. Fix 1 — Floating WhatsApp Button  [CRITICAL]
Why: Saudi clients use WhatsApp for all business communication. They will not fill a contact form. A 
floating WhatsApp button means they can contact Softhrive with one tap from any page.
Exact Implementation
• Position: Fixed — bottom right corner — z-index above everything
• Size: 60px x 60px circle — large enough to tap easily on mobile
• Color: WhatsApp green — #25D366
• Icon: WhatsApp logo SVG — white on green background
• Visible on: Every single page — homepage, service pages, portfolio, contact
• Mobile behavior: Always visible — never hidden on mobile
WhatsApp Link Format
https://wa.me/[YOUR_WHATSAPP_NUMBER]?text=Hi%20Softhrive%20%E2%80%94%20I%20saw
%20your%20ad%20and%20I%20am%20interested%20in%20a%20Shopify%20store
Pre-filled Message
The WhatsApp link should pre-fill this message so Softhrive knows the lead came from the campaign:
Hi Softhrive — I saw your ad and I am interested in a Shopify store
Add your actual WhatsApp business number to the link before going live.
